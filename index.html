How To Live Stream 24/7 on YouTube?

To live stream 24/7 on YouTube, you need to follow a few key steps:

  1. Create a YouTube channel
  2. Connect Upstream.so and YouTube with your Stream key
  3. Upload videos & design your live stream
  4. Start your 24/7 live stream
Step by step guide

How To Find your YouTube Stream Key?

To find your YouTube stream key, follow these steps:

  1. Navigate to YouTube Studio
  2. Select the Go Live option under the Create dropdown button
  3. Click Stream → Stream settings to find your stream key
  4. Copy and paste your stream key into Upstream.so
